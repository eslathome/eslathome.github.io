Introduction

English as a Second Language (ESL) students are those that come from diverse backgrounds and cultures but they share a common goal: becoming mostly fluent in English so that they would be able to communicate effectively and integrate themselves in the U.S.

Why Houston?

There is a large quantity of people in Houston who aren't native English speakers. This diverse population increases the demand for effective ESL programs to support language learners.
