Stages of ESL Learning

  • Silent Period: Learners absorb information.
  • Early Production: Learners begin to learn simple words and phrases.
  • Speech Emergence: Learners start using sentences, opinions, and questions.
  • Intermediate Fluency: Learners become more fluent and can express themselves better.

However...

Unfortunately, this ideal progression often doesn't happen as expected. Public schools frequently lack the necessary funding for strong ESL programs, and many students whose first language is not English do not attend private schools where more resources might be available.
